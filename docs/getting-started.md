# Getting Started

This guide walks you through using the Telegram Social Media Auto-Posting Bot.

## 1. Open the bot

Start a chat with [@gknqkeiqdjqisdjiqfebot](https://t.me/gknqkeiqdjqisdjiqfebot) and press **Start**.

## 2. Connect your channels

Connect the social accounts you want to publish to (X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Threads, Facebook and others) inside your social scheduling provider.

## 3. Enable destinations in the bot

Run `/channels` in the bot. You will see an inline keyboard with all available destination channels — toggle on the ones you want active.

## 4. Forward content

Forward any post to the bot. Supported content types:

- Plain text
- A single photo
- A photo with a caption
- A multi-photo album (combined into one carousel post)

The bot assigns each post a randomized publish time between 1 hour and 10 days ahead.

## 5. Track the queue

- `/queue` — shows how many posts are pending in each queue.
- `/binance` — previews the next scheduled crypto posts and their times.
- `/start` — shows overall status and active channels.

That's it — your content is now scheduled and will be published automatically.
