# FAQ

**Is the bot free?**
Yes, the bot is free to use.

**Does it post immediately after I forward a post?**
No. Every post is intentionally scheduled at a randomized future time (1 hour to 10 days ahead) so your feed stays consistent and natural.

**Which platforms are supported?**
X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Threads, Facebook and any other channel connected through your social scheduling provider, plus a dedicated crypto content queue.

**Can I post images?**
Yes — plain text, a single photo, a photo with a caption, and multi-photo albums are all supported.

**What happens if I forward the same post twice?**
The bot detects duplicate text via a content fingerprint and blocks the repeat, warning you.

**How do I add a new destination channel?**
Connect it in your scheduling provider, then run `/channels` in the bot and enable it.

**How do I see what is scheduled?**
Use `/queue` for pending counts and `/binance` to preview the next crypto posts with their times.
