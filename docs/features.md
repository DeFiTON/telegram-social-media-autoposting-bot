# Features

## Multi-platform cross-posting

Publish one piece of content to many networks at once — X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Threads, Facebook and any other channel connected in your scheduling provider.

## Forward-to-publish workflow

No forms, no copy-paste. Forward a Telegram post to the bot and it is queued for publication automatically.

## Randomized scheduling

Every post receives a random publish time from 1 hour up to 10 days in the future. This keeps your feed active and natural instead of dumping everything at once.

## Crypto content queue

A separate queue is dedicated to crypto-focused social content, with its own scheduler that distributes posts evenly over time.

## Carousel / album support

Send a Telegram album of several photos and the bot groups them into a single multi-image post.

## Duplicate protection

The bot fingerprints the text of every post. If you accidentally forward the same content twice, it is blocked and you are warned.

## Channel management

Use `/channels` to enable or disable individual destination channels with inline buttons. Changes apply immediately.

## Queue visibility

Check pending counts with `/queue` and preview upcoming scheduled posts with `/binance`.
